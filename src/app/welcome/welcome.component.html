Welcome, everyone. Root is a comfortable yoga studio environment that students – whether experienced yogis or curious beginners – can happily call home. Our yoga and movement classes are open to every fitness level and can accommodate any level of practice.

Movement at Root prioritizes traditional practices that balance the mind and body. Movement supports healthy circulation, keeps the connective tissues loose and flexible, and improves mood and mental clarity

hatha

energy **

The foundation of all yoga. Focuses on alignment and breath - moving at a mindful pace that can aid in healing from injury and finding overall balance.

vinyasa

energy ***

A dynamic practice which integrates synchronized breath with a flowing sequence - to increase concentration, muscular strength, and inner grace.

power vinyasa

energy *****

A more intense practice, which takes place in a heated studio. Encourages strength, detoxification and will leave you feeling energized.

family yoga

energy *

Uses the power of storytelling to explore yoga postures and inspire body awareness for children 2-5 and adults alike.

pilates

energy ****

Improves core strength, muscle control, flexibility, coordination, and body tone. Uses a yoga mat and props to increase fitness and endurance and heighten clarity.

restorative

energy *

Provides healing to the mind and body. Expels daily stress and fatigue. Seeks to achieve physical,mental and emotional relaxation with the aid of props.

slow flow

energy *

A balance between strength and grace. As meditation in motion, it encourages internal awareness and connection to breath.

yin yoga

energy *

A more meditative style of yoga with postures that are held for longer periods of time. Improves circulation and flexibility and cultivates inner awareness

yoga foundations

energy **

Focuses on breath and alignment, refining the transitions between poses and fine-tuning the mechanics of the poses.
